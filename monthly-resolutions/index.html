The Concept

Perfectionism. All or nothing thinking. The overwork/burnout cycle. These are my obstacles to making small, sustainable life changes. So I'm doing an experiment. I will allow myself only one goal at a time. It may change every day, or it may be the same for a month. But every morning when I wake up, I will have only one (realistic, attainable) goal in mind. Once I've achieved my goal, changed my priorities, or integrated a goal as a habit, I can replace it with another. But no doubling up. No long, complex plans. One goal, one goal day at a time.
